About Us

At Inidhu, we are committed to ensuring quality education for all children. In August 2024, we signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the District Collector of Thiruvarur to initiate and implement District Educational Quality Improvement Programs (DEQUIP) in the district. As part of this collaboration, we have been provided with a dedicated space at the District Collectorate to drive our efforts.
