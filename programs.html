Our Programs

Inidhu Education Foundation runs two flagship educational programs aimed at transforming learning outcomes for children.

Early Learner Readiness Program (ELRP)

This program focuses on Early Childhood Care & Education (ECCE) in LKG and UKG. We utilize "learning corners" to promote developmentally appropriate practices, ensuring a stimulating environment for young minds.

Key Focus:

  • Enhancing foundational literacy and numeracy skills.
  • Promoting cognitive and social-emotional development.
  • Creating child-friendly and engaging learning spaces.

Educational Quality Improvement Programme (EQUIP)

EQUIP targets students in classes 7 and 8 in government schools. Its primary goals are to improve academic performance and to prepare students for competitive exams like the National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMS).

Key Objectives:

  • Improving overall academic performance in core subjects.
  • Providing specialized training for the NMMS exam.
  • Building critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
