Success Stories

Witness the real-life transformations made possible through Inidhu Education Foundation's programs.

Meet Harish – A Transformation Story

Harish, a four-year-old child, was previously irregular to school. With the introduction of attractive classroom paintings and play materials in learning corners, he started attending school regularly and began playing collaboratively with friends.

Thanks to the engaging environment created through our Early Learner Readiness Program (ELRP), Harish began showing remarkable progress in learning.

Now, Harish can:

  • Count stones up to 5 accurately.
  • Engage actively in picture reading.
  • Create imaginative storylines using books.

Impact of ELRP Pilot Program

In the academic year 2024–25, we successfully completed the ELRP pilot in one school in Thiruvarur district, impacting 18 students over six months (September 2024 – February 2025).

3X

Improvement in students' learning levels

50%

Children could count 3 stones (up from 6.25%)

30%

More improvement than control school

These results prove that a stimulating learning environment, along with capacity building of teachers and increased parental awareness, can dramatically improve early grade learning outcomes.

“This model is scalable, effective, and ready for further roll-out, promising even wider impact.”

What's Next?

We plan to scale the program to 13 more schools across 2 blocks (Thiruvarur and Koradacherry) in the next academic year (2025–26), reaching 250+ students.

With your support, we can provide:

  • Stimulating learning environments with age-appropriate storybooks, worksheets, and TLMs.
  • Professional development of teachers.
  • Parental awareness programs to support children at home.

With your generous support, we will ensure every child has an equal opportunity to design their future.
